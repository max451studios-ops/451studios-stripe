Frequently asked

Do customers need to opt in?

Yes. SMS regulations require opt-in. We provide opt-in confirmation messages and a "STOP to unsubscribe" option built in.

Can I send to my existing customer list?

Only if they've opted in to receive texts from you. Use our bulk import to upload opted-in numbers.

What SMS costs are included?

The $9/mo covers the software. You pay Twilio directly for SMS sending (~$0.0079/text). Most shops spend under $5/mo.

Can I connect this to my shop software?

Yes. We have a simple API endpoint. When you mark a job as complete, send us a webhook and we'll text the customer automatically.
